Muffins

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup blueberries (or other mix-ins)

Baking Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C) and line a muffin pan with liners.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together milk, oil, and eggs.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients; fold in blueberries.
  5. Fill muffin cups about 2/3 full and bake for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Let cool before serving.
