Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cinnamon
  • 1 cup brown sugar, packed

Baking Method

  1. Warm milk and add yeast; let sit for 5 minutes.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, and salt.
  3. Add melted butter, eggs, and yeast mixture; knead until smooth.
  4. Let rise for 1 hour.
  5. Roll out dough, spread with butter, and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
  6. Roll up and cut into slices; let rise again for 30 minutes.
  7. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 15-20 minutes.
